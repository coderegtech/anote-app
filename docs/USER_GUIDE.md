## Getting Started

### What is AnonNote?

AnonNote is a mobile-optimized anonymous messaging platform that allows you to:
- Receive anonymous messages from anyone with your link
- Participate in global chat conversations
- Post questions and receive anonymous replies
- Share custom prompts with your friends

### Installation

#### As a Web App
1. Open [https://your-deployment-url.vercel.app](https://your-deployment-url.vercel.app) in your mobile browser
2. Tap the browser menu (Safari: Share button, Chrome: Menu)
3. Select "Add to Home Screen"
4. Name it "AnonNote" and tap "Add"
5. The app icon will appear on your home screen

#### First Launch
1. Open the app
2. You'll see the splash screen with the AnonNote logo
3. Tap "Get Started!"
4. Enter a unique username (3-20 characters)
5. (Optional) Upload a profile picture
6. Start using the app!

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Is AnonNote really anonymous?**
A: Yes! Messages sent to your inbox are completely anonymous. The sender's identity is never stored or revealed. However, global chat and questions show your username.

**Q: Can I find out who sent me a message?**
A: No, anonymous messages are truly anonymous. There's no way to track who sent them.

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes. We use Firebase for authentication and storage. Your data is encrypted and secure. We don't sell or share your information.

**Q: Do I need an email to sign up?**
A: No! Registration is anonymous. You only need to choose a username.

**Q: Can I use AnonNote on desktop?**
A: Yes, but it's optimized for mobile devices. The experience is best on phones.

---

### Account & Profile

**Q: How do I change my username?**
A: Usernames are currently permanent. If you need to change it, you'll need to create a new account.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Yes. Tap menu ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Delete Account. This is permanent and cannot be undone.

**Q: What if my username is taken?**
A: Try variations like adding numbers or underscores. Usernames must be unique.

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**
A: Yes, but you'll need to sign out and create a new account for each one.

---

### Messages & Inbox

**Q: How many messages can I receive?**
A: There's no limit! Your inbox can store unlimited messages.

**Q: Can I reply to anonymous messages?**
A: No, anonymous messages are one-way. You cannot identify or reply to the sender.

**Q: Do messages expire?**
A: No, messages remain in your inbox until you delete them.

**Q: Can I report inappropriate messages?**
A: Yes, use the report button (flag icon) on any message. We review all reports.

---

### Sharing & Privacy

**Q: Who can see my messages?**
A: Only you can see messages sent to your inbox. They're completely private.

**Q: What if I share my link accidentally?**
A: Delete the post/message where you shared it. You cannot change your link URL without creating a new account.

**Q: Can people spam me with messages?**
A: While anyone with your link can send messages, you can delete them instantly and report abuse.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The app won't load. What do I do?**
A: 
1. Check your internet connection
2. Close and reopen the app
3. Clear your browser cache
4. Try a different browser
5. Contact support if issues persist

**Q: Messages aren't showing up.**
A: 
1. Tap the refresh button
2. Check your internet connection
3. Wait a few seconds and try again
4. Log out and log back in

**Q: I can't upload a profile picture.**
A: 
1. Ensure image is under 5MB
2. Use JPG or PNG format
3. Check browser permissions for file access
4. Try a different image

## Troubleshooting

### App Won't Open

**Symptoms**: White screen, blank page, or immediate crash

**Solutions**:
1. Clear browser cache and cookies
2. Update your browser to latest version
3. Try opening in incognito/private mode
4. Reinstall the app from home screen
5. Use a different browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox)

---

### Can't Sign Up

**Symptoms**: Username won't save, stuck on registration

**Solutions**:
1. Check username meets requirements (3-20 chars)
2. Try a different username
3. Ensure internet connection is stable
4. Clear browser cache
5. Try again in a few minutes

---

### Messages Not Loading

**Symptoms**: Inbox shows empty or spinning loader

**Solutions**:
1. Pull to refresh
2. Check internet connection
3. Wait 30 seconds for auto-refresh
4. Log out and log back in
5. Clear app cache

---

### Chat Not Working

**Symptoms**: Can't send messages, messages not appearing

**Solutions**:
1. Check internet connection
2. Refresh the page
3. Ensure message isn't empty
4. Try logging out and back in
5. Wait a minute and try again

---

### Profile Picture Won't Upload

**Symptoms**: Upload fails or image doesn't appear

**Solutions**:
1. Use JPG or PNG format only
2. Ensure image is under 5MB
3. Try a smaller/compressed image
4. Check browser file permissions
5. Try uploading from a different device

---

### Getting Error Messages

**Common Errors**:

**"Network Error"**
- Check internet connection
- Try switching between WiFi and mobile data
- Wait and try again

**"Invalid Username"**
- Username too short/long
- Contains invalid characters
- Already taken by another user

**"Upload Failed"**
- File too large
- Unsupported format
- Network timeout - try again

**"Authentication Error"**
- Log out and log back in
- Clear cookies
- Contact support if persists
